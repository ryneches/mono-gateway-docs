# 10G Fiber

ISPs deploy a variety of technologies for 10G fiberoptic service. Depending on
your ISP, service level and region of the world, you will need to use
a different SFP+ module to connect your router directly.

## What is an ONU?

- **ONU** : Optical Network Unit. This is usually the customer-premises device.
- **ONT** : Optical Network Terminal. Synonym for ONU.
- **OLT** : Optical Line Terminal. This is usually the network service provider device.
